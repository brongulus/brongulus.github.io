Morphosyntactic Tagging with a Meta-BiLSTM Model over Context Sensitive Token Encodings - An Overview

Abstract RNN leads to advances in speech tagging accuracy Zeman et al Common thing among models, rich initial word encodings. (What does encoding, maybe related to RNN) Encodings are composed of recurrent character-based representation with learned and pre-trained word embeddings. (What are embeddings here) Problem with the encodings, context restriced to a single word hence only via subsequent recurrent layers the word information is processed. The paper deals with models that use RNN with sentence-level context.
